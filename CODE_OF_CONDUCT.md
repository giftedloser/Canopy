# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Canopy is maintained as a respectful, technical, and collaborative project.
Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and argue from evidence.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Avoid harassment, hostility, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassing, insulting, or demeaning language
- Threats, intimidation, or unwanted contact
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission
- Disruptive behavior that prevents productive collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and other public collaboration channels related to Canopy.
